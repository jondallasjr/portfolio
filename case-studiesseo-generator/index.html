SEO Article Generator for Property Portal

Client: Largest Property Index in Country Platform: Custom Web App + CMS Duration: 4 months

The Challenge

Despite being the largest property index in their country, the client consistently ranked between 4th-6th position in Google searches. They needed to increase search rankings but had limited content production capacity.

The Solution

I built a comprehensive system that identifies opportunities, researches competitors, leverages proprietary data, and generates high-quality, unique content.

The System

  1. Keyword Research: Identify high-ROI keywords (high traffic, low competition)
  2. Competitor Analysis: Scrape top 10 articles for target keywords
  3. Gap Analysis: Determine what's missing from competitor content
  4. Database Integration: Query 10,000+ property database for unique metrics
  5. Google Maps API: Pull nearby schools, cafes, parks, libraries
  6. Dynamic Writing Guidelines: Apply appropriate style for article type
  7. Content Generation: 4,000-word articles with unique data points
  8. Quality Assurance: AI checks if article followed all guidelines
  9. Human Review: Final editorial pass
  10. CMS Integration: Track generation status and performance metrics

Why This Worked

  • Original data matters: Google rewards unique information, not regurgitated content
  • E-E-A-T compliance: Articles demonstrate experience, expertise, authority, trust
  • Comprehensive coverage: Articles answer questions competitors miss
  • Scalable process: Can maintain quality while producing at scale
