Resume Rewriter for Career Platform

Client: Career Coaching Platform Platform: Coda.io with AI APIs Duration: 3 months

The Challenge

A career platform was spending $240 per resume rewrite using human writers. With growing demand, this wasn't sustainable. But they couldn't compromise on qualityâ€”resumes needed to be accurate, tailored, and compelling.

The Solution

I built an AI-powered system in Coda.io that automates the research and drafting while keeping humans in the loop for quality control.

The Workflow

  1. Parse Resume: Extract structured data from uploaded resume
  2. Find LinkedIn: AI identifies and scrapes candidate's LinkedIn profile
  3. Determine Target Job: Analyze resume + LinkedIn to identify career goals
  4. Research Live Postings: Google search for relevant job listings
  5. Keyword Analysis: Identify which keywords, metrics, and achievements matter most
  6. Gap Analysis: Find what's missing from the current resume
  7. Follow-Up Questions: Generate email with specific questions for client
  8. Apply Writing Guidelines: Determine appropriate style and tone
  9. Rewrite Resume: AI generates new version
  10. Quality Assurance: AI checks thoroughness, accuracy, and factual claims
  11. Human Review: Career coach reviews and finalizes (this is critical!)

The Results

87% Cost Reduction

From $240 to $30 per resume

Human Touch Maintained

Final review ensures quality and accuracy

15% Conversion

Free version drives paid upgrades

Key Insights

  • AI makes mistakes: That's why we built QA into the system itself before human review
  • Factual accuracy matters: We verify all claims against source documents
  • Human oversight is non-negotiable: Especially for high-stakes content like resumes
  • Multi-step processes work: Breaking the task into stages improves quality
